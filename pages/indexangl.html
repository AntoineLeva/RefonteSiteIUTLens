    IUT Department

    IT IUT

    In order to facilitate an insertion into the professional life, the students are trained in computer science and teamwork, training they can receive in the various IT departments of the IUT of France. These establishments offer a complete training, assisted by professionals and professors, present to bring their knowledge and experience to the students, in search of new knowledge. 3 major fields allow to conceive a necessary teaching in computer science (in OTC or Professional license):

    Analysis and Design :

    Analysis of the needs of the client in order to answer in the best possible way in order to meet his expectations, following the realization of a computer processing.
    Open-mindedness, ability to reflect and synthesize, communication...

    Algorithmics and Programming :

    Set up a succession of orders in order to achieve an effective solution, from processing to coding.
    Logical mind, reflection, technical correctness...

    System and Network Architecture :

    Study of the functioning of a computer, its operating system and its networks.
    Need to be patient, curious, thirsty for novelties...

    IT IUT

    Other general subjects are added to these few teachings such as mathematics, economics and management, English or even Communication. Thus, the evaluation is carried out on a multitude of different subjects, and not only on a theoretical but also practical aspect via tutored projects, internship in a company, promoting in particular the contribution of professional experience, and allowing a Assessment of the knowledge and skills acquired during the training.
