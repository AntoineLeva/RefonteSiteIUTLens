    IUT and Map

    The IUT, with a number of 115 in French territory, allows a strong anchorage in the local and regional economic environment. Following the introduction of programmes adapted to the professional world as well as the appearance of diplomas corresponding to the new looms, the IUT has a certain recognition with regard to its quality of teaching, and for more than 40 years. The training offered allows students to enter the world of work within 6 months of graduation, in particular through a presence in most sectors (secondary and tertiary), and also in collaboration with Professionals bringing their experience to the students. In order to meet the maximum demands of students and professionals wishing to specialize or resume their studies, new pedagogical modes are born, through the diversification of trainings and diplomas. In addition, studies abroad, and internships, are offered to a number of students, through partnerships with foreign universities.
