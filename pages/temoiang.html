    Testimony of Alexandre Leva

    I had started studying medicine. It wasn't a great success, I didn't like these studies. I therefore reoriented myself in DU Tremplin informatique at IUT A in Lille. Following this rebound semester, I arrived at the IUT of Lens for the DUT in IT. The training was fairly general and it was accessible to beginners and thanks to my DU springboard I immediately felt very comfortable with the training. I discovered that the IT world was bigger than I imagined. The teachers brought me a lot of different knowledge and their enthusiasm became mine. I wanted to continue after the DUT and now I am in engineering school at IMT Lille Douai.

    Currently, I am a work-study, I work for the company Agymat. It is a small company (8 people) that sells building materials on the internet. The subjects taught at IUT gave me, I think, a chance to succeed in engineering school, and to become a good professional.
