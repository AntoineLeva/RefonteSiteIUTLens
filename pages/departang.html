    IUT Department

    During his training, the student will acquire the knowledge, know-how, basic concepts and method of work that will benefit him in his professional pursuit.


    The OTC :

    Any person with a computer-related OTC is considered to be a senior technician with the ability to participate in the design, realization and implementation of computer systems, but may choose to pursue its Studies.


    The Pro License:

    SIL Licensees (computer systems and software) are considered to be specialists with the ability to participate in a software development project, and ultimately to take full responsibility for them.


    Validation of the acquis:

    In order to obtain a diploma, title or Certificate of professional qualification, it is possible to have his experience recognized (professional or not).
